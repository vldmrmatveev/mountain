About Us

Mountain Travel is the largest and longest running annual gathering of mountain travel professionals in the world. More than 1 350 representatives from 40 countries establish relationships, build their business, pursue professional development and create a stronger mountain community.

MT has transformed into a truly global event. Buyers and suppliers from over thirty five countries now attend each year. Establish new relationships and improve your existing business. Join other leaders of the mountain travel industry to learn from each other and create the MT community of mountain travel experts.

MT provides you with the ability to have pre-scheduled high-quality meetings with more than 400 mountain destination sales and marketing executives. You'll interact with representatives from all over the US and Canada as well as an increasing number of suppliers from Europe, Asia and South America.

Rocky

The Rocky Mountains, also known as the Rockies, are a major mountain range in western North America.

3865$ $2123

The Rocky Mountains formed 80 million to 55 million years ago during the Laramide orogeny, in which a number of plates began sliding underneath the North American plate. The angle of subduction was shallow, resulting in a broad belt of mountains running down western North America.
